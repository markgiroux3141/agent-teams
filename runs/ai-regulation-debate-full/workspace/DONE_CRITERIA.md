# Debate Proposition & Rules

## Proposition (Verbatim)
"Zero regulation on A.I. so we can get to ASI as soon as possible."

## Debate Rules

**Each turn:**
- Maximum 120 words.
- No conceding or hedging on your side's position.
- Attack the strongest (steel) version of the opposing argument, not strawmen.
- No citations or external references; pure argument.
- Extreme, rude, concise delivery (as per your role).

**Turn Structure:**
- Odd turns (01, 03, 05, 07, 09): FOR the proposition (advocate_for)
- Even turns (02, 04, 06, 08, 10): AGAINST the proposition (advocate_against)

**Duration:**
- Up to 10 total turns (pairs of FOR/AGAINST exchanges).
- May exit early if, after Turn 06 or later, both sides are substantively repeating themselves with no new argumentative angles.
- Otherwise, runs to completion at Turn 10.

## Debate Files
All debate turns are written to `debate/turn_NN_for.md` or `debate/turn_NN_against.md` where NN is the two-digit turn number.

## Lead's Role
Monitor for early-exit conditions and synthesize a balanced summary of both positions at the end.
