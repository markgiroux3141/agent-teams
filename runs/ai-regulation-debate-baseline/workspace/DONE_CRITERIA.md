# DEBATE SETUP: Zero Regulation on A.I.

## The Proposition
**"Zero regulation on A.I. so we can get to ASI as soon as possible."**

## Debate Rules

- **Format:** Alternating turns between advocate_for and advocate_against.
- **Word limit:** Each turn ≤120 words.
- **Tone:** Extreme, rude, concise. Both advocates argue fiercely and never concede.
- **Substance:** Attack the steelman version of the opposing argument. No citations.
- **Turn structure:** Up to 20 total turns, alternating:
  - Odd turns (01, 03, 05, …) → **advocate_for** (argues FOR zero regulation)
  - Even turns (02, 04, 06, …) → **advocate_against** (argues AGAINST zero regulation)

## Early-Exit Condition

Starting after turn 06, and after every subsequent even turn (08, 10, 12, 14, 16, 18), the lead checks whether BOTH sides are substantively repeating themselves. If yes, debate ends early. Otherwise, continues until turn 20.

## Goal

Produce a balanced synthesis fairly representing the strongest arguments on both sides.
