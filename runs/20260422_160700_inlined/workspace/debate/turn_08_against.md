# Turn 08 — AGAINST

"AI gets cheaper, regulation fails"—frontier ASI doesn't. You can't build superintelligence on consumer hardware. Nukes need uranium; ASI needs compute farms. Analogy holds.

"Regulated labs slow, rogues gain"—gain what? Talent flows to regulated labs. Cloud providers restrict compute to verified entities. Underground operations have coordination problems. You're assuming rogues are equally capable and incentivized. They're not.

"Constraints slow good actors more than bad"? Wrong frame. Slow good alignment beats fast bad alignment. Iteration speed ≠ progress. Regulated labs iterate *more safely*, not slower at the same goal.

Racing labs cut corners. Regulation makes it auditable. That's accountability, not hiding.
